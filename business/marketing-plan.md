# Marketing Plan: forecast-loom

## Positioning
**Primary:** "Scalable time-series forecasting for data scientists and analysts."
**Alternatives:**
1. "Efficient, large-scale time-series forecasting for complex datasets."
2. "Integrated time-series forecasting for seamless data science workflows."
3. "High-performance forecasting for data-driven decision-making."

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profiles)
1. **Data Scientist Dan**
   - **Daily Job:** Works on predictive modeling and data analysis for a mid-sized tech company.
   - **Budget:** $5,000 - $10,000 per year for tools and software.
2. **Analyst Amy**
   - **Daily Job:** Focuses on financial forecasting and trend analysis for a financial services firm.
   - **Budget:** $3,000 - $8,000 per year for analytical tools.
3. **Engineer Eric**
   - **Daily Job:** Manages data pipelines and forecasting models for an e-commerce platform.
   - **Budget:** $7,000 - $15,000 per year for data infrastructure and tools.

## Channels
1. **LinkedIn Ads**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $50 - $100 per acquisition.
   - **Rationale:** Targets data scientists, analysts, and engineers with precise job titles and interests.
2. **Google Ads**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $30 - $80 per acquisition.
   - **Rationale:** Captures search intent for time-series forecasting and related keywords.
3. **Content Marketing (Blog, Webinars)**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $20 - $60 per acquisition.
   - **Rationale:** Builds thought leadership and attracts organic traffic through valuable content.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1-4:** Blog posts on time-series forecasting best practices, case studies, and comparative analyses.
- **Week 5-8:** Webinars featuring industry experts discussing forecasting challenges and solutions.
- **Week 9-12:** Customer success stories, how-to guides, and detailed product demos.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize product features, create landing page, and set up marketing automation.
- **D-0:** Official launch with press release, social media announcements, and initial ad campaigns.
- **D+30:** First customer onboarding, gather feedback, and optimize marketing messages.
- **D+90:** Expand ad campaigns, launch advanced features, and scale customer acquisition efforts.

## Success Metrics
- **DAU (Daily Active Users):** Aim for 500 DAU by D+90.
- **$MRR Target:** Achieve $20,000 in Monthly Recurring Revenue by D+90.
